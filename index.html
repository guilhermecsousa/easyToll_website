About the project

 This project arises in the context of PASMO (Plataforma Aberta para o desenvolvimento e experimentação de Soluções para a Mobilidade) from the need to develop platforms to support Intelligent Transport Systems and Vehicle Communications.

 The emergence and evolution of cryptocurrencies and transactions in DLT (Distributed Ledger Technology or distributed registration p.e Blockchain) also determinants for the future of digital transactions

 It arises from the desire to provide safer, more efficient, reliable, intelligent and automatic services with regard to mobility and what comes from it.
